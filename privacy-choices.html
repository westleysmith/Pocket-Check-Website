Your Privacy Choices

Effective Date: April 21, 2026 Last Updated: April 21, 2026

This page summarizes the choices and rights you have over the personal information Pocket Check holds about you. It applies to all users, and includes additional rights required by California, Colorado, Virginia, Connecticut, Utah, and other state privacy laws. For the full list of what we collect and how, see our Privacy Policy.

1. We Do Not Sell or Share Your Personal Information

Pocket Check does not sell your personal information and does not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. We do not receive money or other valuable consideration for your data.

Your financial data is used only to power your own experience in the App (affordability verdicts, AI coaching, budgeting, net worth tracking). It is not shared with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing networks.

2. Your Rights

Regardless of where you live, you can exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Delete: request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Correct: request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Withdraw consent: disconnect any linked banks or disable push notifications at any time from the App

Residents of California, Colorado, Virginia, Connecticut, Utah, and other states with applicable laws additionally have the right to:

  • Opt out of sale or sharing of personal information (we do not engage in either, so there is nothing to opt out of, but you may confirm this with us)
  • Opt out of targeted advertising (we do not engage in targeted advertising)
  • Opt out of profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects (we do not engage in this kind of profiling)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising any of these rights

3. How to Exercise Your Rights

All requests can be submitted by emailing pocketcheckapp@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Pocket Check account. In the subject line, note the type of request (for example, "Access Request", "Deletion Request", or "Correction Request").

In the body, include:

  • Your full name
  • The email associated with your Pocket Check account
  • The specific right you are exercising
  • Any detail relevant to the request (e.g. what you want corrected)

We will acknowledge your request within 10 business days and complete it within 30 days. Complex requests may take up to 60 additional days, in which case we will notify you and explain the delay.

4. Verification

To protect your privacy, we will verify that the person making a request is the account holder. We do this by matching the requester's email to the account email and, if needed, asking a confirmation question about recent account activity. We will not fulfill requests we cannot verify.

5. Authorized Agents

You may designate an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf. The agent must provide signed permission from you, and we may still verify your identity directly before acting on the request.

6. Appeals

If we deny a privacy request, you may appeal by replying to our decision email. We will respond to appeals within 45 days with our final decision and, where applicable, information on how to contact your state's Attorney General if you remain dissatisfied.

7. Children Under 16

Pocket Check is not intended for use by anyone under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 or sell or share personal information of users under 16 under any applicable state law.
