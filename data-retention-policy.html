This Data Retention and Disposal Policy describes how Pocket Check LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles the retention and deletion of data collected through the Pocket Check mobile application (the "App"). This policy supplements our Privacy Policy.

1. Overview

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it using secure methods.

We do not retain your data indefinitely. Our goal is to minimize the data we hold and the time we hold it.

2. Data We Retain

2.1 On-Device Data

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Account balances and transaction history retrieved via Plaid
  • Budget targets, income figures, and bill information you enter manually
  • AI chat history between you and the financial coach

This data persists on your device until you clear it from Settings or uninstall the App.

2.2 Server-Side Data

The only data stored on our backend servers is:

  • Your Plaid access token, which allows us to retrieve your financial data on your behalf
  • Your device push notification token (if you have granted notification permission)

3. Retention Periods

Plaid Access Token

Retained on our servers for as long as you have an active bank connection within the App. Deleted immediately upon disconnecting your bank account from within the App, or upon account deletion request.

Push Notification Token

Retained for as long as you have notifications enabled. Deleted when you disable notifications or request account deletion.

On-Device Financial Data

Controlled entirely by you. Retained on your device until you clear app data in Settings or uninstall the App. We have no access to or copies of this data.

Crash and Performance Logs

Anonymized diagnostic data may be retained for up to 90 days for the purpose of identifying and resolving technical issues.

4. Data Disposal

When data reaches the end of its retention period or is requested for deletion, we use the following disposal methods:

  • Server-side records (Plaid tokens, notification tokens) are permanently deleted from our database and are not recoverable
  • On-device data is cleared using the secure erase functionality provided by the iOS operating system
  • Anonymized diagnostic logs are purged on a rolling 90-day schedule

We do not archive or back up personal data beyond what is necessary to run our services. Deleted data is not retained in backups beyond 30 days.

5. Your Deletion Rights

5.1 Disconnect Your Bank

You can disconnect your bank account at any time from within the App. Upon disconnection, your Plaid access token is immediately and permanently deleted from our servers.

5.2 Clear On-Device Data

You can delete all locally stored financial data, chat history, and app settings from the Settings screen within the App at any time.

5.3 Request Full Deletion

To request deletion of any remaining server-side data, contact us at pocketcheckapp@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days and confirm completion.

6. Third-Party Data

Pocket Check relies on third-party services that maintain their own data retention practices:

  • Plaid Technologies: Retains data per their own privacy policy at plaid.com/legal
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Does not retain your data beyond the duration of each individual API request
  • Railway: Hosts our backend infrastructure. Server logs may be retained per Railway's own policies at railway.app/legal/privacy

We encourage you to review the privacy and retention policies of these third parties directly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you through the App.
